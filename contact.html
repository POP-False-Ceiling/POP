Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about our services

How long does a typical ceiling installation take?

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard residential ceiling installation typically takes 3-7 days, while more complex designs or larger commercial projects may take 2-3 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free on-site consultations and estimates for all our services. Our expert will visit your location, understand your requirements, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.

What materials do you use for your ceiling installations?

We use premium quality materials sourced from trusted suppliers. For POP work, we use high-grade Plaster of Paris. For false ceilings, we offer options including gypsum boards, PVC panels, metal panels, and wooden finishes. All materials are selected for their durability, appearance, and suitability for your specific requirements.

Do you offer any warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide a 1-year warranty on our workmanship and installation. This covers any issues related to the installation process. Material warranties vary depending on the manufacturer, and we'll provide you with all warranty information before the project begins.

Can you work with my interior designer or architect?

Absolutely! We regularly collaborate with interior designers, architects, and contractors. We're happy to work with your design team to ensure that the ceiling solutions align perfectly with the overall design vision for your space.
